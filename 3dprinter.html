My 3D Printer Build

Early in 2021 I bought a Creality Ender 3 v2 3D printer. It's still somewhat of a work in progress. Here I document the tweaks, changes, additions, and surrounding artifacts.

Other elements

Raspberry Pi 4 running OctoPi (a custom distribution of OctoPrint for the Raspberry Pi); custom breakout board in the HAT format (though not technically HAT-compatible, as it does not provide an EEPROM to read) to bring out GPIO pins to a custom control box, and to access sensors. Utilises the Enclosure plugin (info on Thingiverse).

Smart plug flashed with Tasmota, to allow OctoPrint to shut the printer down when not in use to save power.

Smoke alarm mounted to roof of enclosure.

LEDBERG light strips mounted to roof of enclosure.

PSU moved outside of enclosure, short cable runs to a barrier strip inside enclosure to pass power to the printer. DANGER: can cause major electric damage if not careful! Use barrier strip cover to minimise chance of short.

Paper towel holder mounted below enclosure, to allow quick bed cleaning using spray IPA.
